Our Mission.

Stockton University has been greatly affected by the Covid-19 crisis. Remote learning has presented a new set of challenges for the entire student body, staff and faculty alike. This tool aims to perform a platform for the Stockton community to quickly and accurately assess their symptoms and provide insight to make smart travel decisions.

  • How we do it.

How it all works.

You will be presented with a list of symptoms. Research shows certain clusters of symptoms are strongly related to Covid-19. Select any symptoms you may be having and our tool will calculate how closely your symptoms match with the recognized clusters and provide you with a risk score. You may be prompted to provide your frequently visited campus locations, this data will always remain private and is used to improve the tool. After the survey you will be shown an interactive map of campus that displays the "hot-spot" locations around the university.
